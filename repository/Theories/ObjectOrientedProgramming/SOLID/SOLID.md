# SOLID原则

---

SOLID原则是面向对象程序设计中的5大指导性原则的简称, 它们分别是:

- [**S**ingle Responsibility Principle - 单一职责原则](/repository/Theories/ObjectOrientedProgramming/SOLID/SingleResponsibilityPrinciple.md#singleresponsibilityprinciple---单一职责原则)
- [**O**pen Closed Principle - 开闭原则](/repository/Theories/ObjectOrientedProgramming/SOLID/OpenClosedPrinciple.md#openclosedprinciple---开闭原则)
- [**L**iskov Substitution Principle - 里氏代换原则](/repository/Theories/ObjectOrientedProgramming/SOLID/LiskovSubstitutionPrinciple.md#liskovsubstitutionprinciple---里氏代换原则)
- [**I**nterface Segregation Principle - 接口隔离原则](/repository/Theories/ObjectOrientedProgramming/SOLID/InterfaceSegregationPrinciple.md#interfacesegregationprinciple---接口隔离原则)
- [**D**ependency Inversion Principle - 依赖倒置原则](/repository/Theories/ObjectOrientedProgramming/SOLID/DependencyInversionPrinciple.md#dependencyinversionprinciple---依赖倒置原则)
